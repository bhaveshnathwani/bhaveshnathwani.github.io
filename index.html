About

At Canopy, I lead our data strategy end-to-end, owning the entire data function as we scale rapidly. From designing robust data infrastructure to driving strategic insights, I ensure that data is not just an asset but a core driver of business growth.

I oversee data analytics and our internal Data Team, embedding a data-driven culture across the organisation. My focus is on aligning data strategy with business objectives; whether through advanced data visualisation, streamlined reporting, or delivering insights that enhance decision-making across teams.

With a strong foundation in Mathematics and an MSc in Applied Data Science & AI underway, I specialise in turning complex datasets into actionable strategies. Passionate about the intersection of data and business impact, I thrive on transforming raw data into meaningful narratives that drive smarter decisions and long-term success.
